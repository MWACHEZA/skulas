Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really a free trial? ▾
Yes! Every plan includes a full 30-day free trial. No credit card is required to start.
Can I change plans later? ▾
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your admin dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.
Is our school's data private from other schools? ▾
Yes. ACADEX uses a strict multi-tenant architecture. Each school has its own isolated data environment. No school can ever see another school's data.
What school types does ACADEX support? ▾
ACADEX supports all school types: Primary, Secondary, Colleges, Private Day Schools, Private Boarding Schools, Public Day Schools, Public Boarding Schools, and Combined Schools.
Do I need technical expertise to set up? ▾
Not at all. Fill in the onboarding form and your school is ready in minutes. Our support team can assist with data migration and staff onboarding at no extra charge.
