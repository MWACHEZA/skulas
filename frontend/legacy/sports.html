Our Sports Programs

At Embakwe High School, we believe in the holistic development of our students. Our sports programs are designed to instill discipline, teamwork, and leadership skills.

Age Groups

Boys Soccer Team

Our boys soccer team has consistently ranked among the top teams in the region. They train three times a week under the guidance of Coach Michael Thompson.

Girls Soccer Team

The girls soccer team has shown remarkable improvement over the past few years. They have qualified for the national championships twice in the last three years.

Recent Achievements

Regional Champions 2023

Boys team won the regional championship for the third consecutive year.

National Runners-up 2022

Girls team finished second in the national school championships.

Best Goalkeeper Award

David Moyo won the best goalkeeper award at the inter-school tournament.

Our Coaches

Boys Soccer Coach
