Clubs & Activities

Discover the diverse range of clubs and activities at Embakwe High School that help our students develop leadership skills, pursue their passions, and build lasting friendships.

Christian Life Community (CLC)

The Christian Life Community club focuses on spiritual growth, community service, and living out Christian values in everyday life. Members engage in prayer, reflection, and service projects.

Meets every Tuesday

Advisor: Mr. James Moyo

Open to all students

Join Club

Public Speaking Club

Our Public Speaking Club helps students develop confidence and effective communication skills. Members practice speeches, debates, and presentations in a supportive environment.

Meets every Wednesday

Advisor: Mrs. Sarah Ndlovu

Open to all students

Join Club

Debate Club

The Debate Club challenges students to think critically and argue persuasively on various topics. Members participate in interschool competitions and develop research and analytical skills.

Meets every Monday

Advisor: Mr. Thomas Sibanda

Open to all students

Join Club

Spelling Bee Club

Our Spelling Bee Club helps students improve their vocabulary, spelling skills, and language proficiency. Members prepare for local and national spelling competitions.

Meets every Thursday

Advisor: Ms. Elizabeth Dube

Open to all students

Join Club

Drama Club

The Drama Club provides opportunities for students to explore acting, directing, and stagecraft. Members produce plays and performances throughout the school year.

Meets twice weekly

Advisor: Mrs. Patricia Ncube

Open to all students

Join Club

Interact Club

The Interact Club is a Rotary-sponsored service club that engages students in community service projects. Members develop leadership skills while making a positive impact in the community.

Open to all students

Join Club

Edmund Rice Society

The Edmund Rice Society promotes the values and vision of Blessed Edmund Rice through service, advocacy, and social justice initiatives. Members work on projects that address local and global issues.

Meets every Wednesday

Advisor: Brother Michael Chuma

Open to all students

School Choir

Our School Choir brings together students with a passion for music and singing. The choir performs at school events, competitions, and community functions.

Religious Activities

Daily Masses

Embakwe High School offers daily masses for students and staff to nurture spiritual growth and provide moments of reflection during busy school days.

Schedule

  • Monday-Friday: 6:30 AM
  • Location: School Chapel
  • Celebrant: School Chaplain

Sunday Mass

Our Sunday masses are a cornerstone of our school's spiritual life, bringing together the entire school community for worship and fellowship.

Schedule

  • Sunday: 8:00 AM
  • Location: Main School Chapel
  • Celebrant: Visiting Priests

Wednesday High School Mass

Wednesday masses are specifically designed for high school students, addressing topics and challenges relevant to teenage spiritual development.

Schedule

  • Wednesday: 2:30 PM
  • Location: High School Chapel
  • Celebrant: School Chaplain
