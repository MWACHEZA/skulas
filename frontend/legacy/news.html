June 15, 2023

Annual Science Fair Winners Announced

Congratulations to all participants in this year's science fair. The creativity and innovation displayed by our students were truly impressive. Special recognition goes to the top three projects:

1st Place: "Renewable Energy Solutions" by Sarah Johnson
2nd Place: "Water Purification System" by Michael Chen
3rd Place: "Biodegradable Plastics" by Emily Rodriguez

June 10, 2023

New Computer Lab Inauguration

The school proudly inaugurated its state-of-the-art computer lab equipped with 30 new computers, high-speed internet, and the latest software. This facility will enhance our students' digital literacy and provide them with essential skills for the future.

The lab was made possible through generous donations from our alumni association and corporate partners.

June 5, 2023

Sports Day Celebration

Students showcased their athletic talents during the annual sports day. Events included track and field, basketball, volleyball, and soccer tournaments. The Blue House emerged as the overall champion with 250 points.

Special congratulations to John Smith who broke the school record in the 100m sprint with a time of 10.85 seconds.

May 28, 2023

Cultural Day Celebrations

Our annual Cultural Day was a vibrant celebration of diversity and heritage. Students presented traditional dances, music, and cuisine from various cultures around the world.

The event fostered cultural appreciation and understanding among our diverse student body. Parents and community members joined in the festivities.

May 20, 2023

End of Term Examination Schedule Released

The schedule for the end-of-term examinations has been released. Exams will run from June 20 to July 5, 2023. Students are advised to prepare adequately and check the detailed timetable on the school notice board.

Study resources and past papers are available in the school library and online learning portal.

May 15, 2023

Debate Team Wins Regional Championship

Our debate team emerged victorious at the Regional Schools Debate Championship. The team demonstrated exceptional critical thinking, research, and public speaking skills throughout the competition.

They will now represent our region at the National Debate Finals scheduled for August 2023.
