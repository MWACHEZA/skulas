Frequently Asked Questions

Are alumni contributions tax-deductible?

Yes, all contributions to Your School are tax-deductible as allowed by law. You will receive an official receipt that can be used for tax purposes.

How are alumni contributions used?

Alumni contributions are used to fund scholarships for deserving students, improve school infrastructure, purchase educational resources, support extracurricular activities, and enhance the overall learning environment at Your School.

Can I specify how my contribution is used?

Yes, you can designate your contribution for a specific purpose such as scholarships, infrastructure development, or educational resources. Simply indicate your preference when making your payment.

How often should I contribute?

The frequency of contributions is entirely up to you. Some alumni prefer annual contributions, while others opt for one-time donations or regular monthly contributions. Any amount and frequency is appreciated.

How can I track my contributions?

You can view your contribution history in the "Payment History" section of your alumni portal. This provides a record of all your contributions, including dates, amounts, and purposes.
