Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the application review process take?
Applications are typically reviewed within 10-14 working days. You will receive a notification via the "Messages" tab and your status on the dashboard will be updated once a decision has been made.
What are the payment options for school fees?
We accept Bank Transfers and Mobile Money. Please refer to the "Fee Structure" page for detailed banking information. Always use the student's name as the reference.
Is boarding compulsory?
Please refer to the school's enrollment policy. Most boarding facilities require full-time residency to ensure participation in academic and extra-curricular life.
Can I edit my application after submission?
Core application details cannot be edited after submission. However, you can update your Profile information or upload supplementary documents at any time.
How should I prepare for the admission interview?
Please bring your original birth certificate and previous result slips. Interviews are informal conversations to get to know you better.
